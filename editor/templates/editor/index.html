Pont

Graphology is the analysis of handwriting to determine an individual's character and personality. During our childhood, we were all taught to write the same letters the same way, but it is evident that no one continues to write exactly the same. As everyone grows up, people gradually alter the shape and sizes of their letters according to their personal preferences.

Pont was created and inspired by these individualised traits that are used to read a person's characteristics and personality. It begins with a sans serif font which then morphs to best suit you !
